Data Analysis on WeRateDogs¶

Introduction¶

WeRateDogs dataset is the tweet archive of Twitter user @dog_rates.WeRateDogs is a Twitter account that rates people's dogs with a humorous comment about the dog. These ratings almost always have a denominator of 10. The dataset is wrangled and is ready for analysis.
